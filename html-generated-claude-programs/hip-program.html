Stop IfPinching in front of hip (possible FAI). Do not force hip flexion past that point.
Normal SensationDeep spreading feeling in back/side of hip. Mild post-session soreness in capsule.
Not NormalSharp stabbing pain, painful clicking, or radiating nerve pain.
PAILs SorenessExpect significant capsule soreness after first PAILs session (Wk 3). This is expected — rest 48–72h before next dedicated session.
